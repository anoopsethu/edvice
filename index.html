Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I know that the tutor I'm connected to is authentic?

    All tutors on Albus are undergraduates of IITs or BITS Pilani. Moreover, our team ensures all the tutors go through an extensive Academic Credential verification process to ensure that students have access to the best pool of tutors round the clock. Rest assured, you will be tutored from the very best.

  • What is the refund policy if I'm not satisfied with my lesson?

    Students get charged by the minute and are charged once the lesson is completed. Our Customer Support team will go through the transcripts of the lesson in question and will refund the student as and when a serious discrepancy is reported.

  • What happens if I'm unable to connect to a tutor instantly?

    We have ensured that this is an unlikely scenario. However, if this were to happen, we have an option for you to schedule a session later and we will ensure you are connected to a tutor at your convenience.

  • Can I schedule a lesson with a tutor of my choice?

    We at Albus believe that all of our tutors are well qualified to give you the best possible teaching experience that will be to your satisfaction. So no, you'll be connected to a tutor who is available at that instant and not necessarily the tutor you want.
